TACOS

Authentic Mexican Street Tacos Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins | Total Time: 50 mins Servings: 4 (12 tacos)


Ingredients:

For Meat:
1.5 lbs skirt steak or chicken thighs
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp smoked paprika
Juice of 2 limes
2 tbsp olive oil
Salt to taste
For Serving:
12 corn tortillas
1 white onion, finely chopped
1 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
2 limes, quartered
1 cup salsa verde or roja
1 avocado, sliced
Crumbled queso fresco (optional)
Radish slices (optional)

Instructions:

1.Marinate Meat:
Combine garlic, spices, lime juice and oil. Coat meat and marinate 15+ mins.
2.Cook Meat:
Grill or pan-sear over high heat 3-4 mins per side. Rest 5 mins, then slice thin.
3.Prep Toppings:
Chop onion and cilantro. Warm tortillas on dry skillet (30 sec per side).
4.Assemble Tacos:
Double-layer tortillas. Add meat, onions, cilantro. Squeeze lime.
5.Serve:
Serve immediately with salsas, avocado, and optional toppings.

Tips:
✔ Warm tortillas properly - they should be pliable, not crispy
✔ Authentic street style: Use two tortillas per taco
✔ Alternative proteins: Al pastor, carnitas, or grilled shrimp
✔ For vegetarian: Substitute with grilled portobello or cauliflower
✔ Make it spicy: Add sliced jalapeños or hotter salsa
✔ Leftover idea: Make taco salad or breakfast tacos next day
