ADOBO

Chicken Adobo Recipe Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 40 mins | Total Time: 50 mins Servings: 4


Ingredients:

2 lbs (1 kg) chicken thighs or drumsticks (bone-in, skin-on for best flavor)
½ cup soy sauce
½ cup white vinegar (or cane vinegar)
1 cup water
6-8 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp whole black peppercorns (or ½ tsp ground pepper)
3-4 bay leaves
1 tbsp brown sugar (optional, for slight sweetness)
2 tbsp cooking oil
1 onion, sliced (optional)

Instructions:

1.Marinate (Optional but Recommended):
In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves.
Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for deeper flavor).
2.Sear the Chicken:
Heat oil in a large pot or pan over medium heat.
Remove chicken from marinade (reserve marinade) and lightly brown on both sides (3-4 mins per side). Set aside.
3.Simmer:
In the same pot, sauté garlic (and onion if using) until fragrant.
Pour in the reserved marinade, water, and vinegar. Do not stir yet (let the vinegar cook off for a minute).
Add back the chicken, bring to a boil, then lower heat to a simmer.
Cover and cook for 25-30 mins, turning chicken occasionally.
4.Reduce the Sauce:
Uncover and simmer for 5-10 mins to thicken the sauce.
Optional: Add brown sugar for a hint of sweetness.
5.Serve:
Enjoy hot with steamed rice! br Best garnished with extra garlic or green onions.

Tips:
✔ Vinegar Tip: Don’t stir right after adding vinegar to avoid a sour taste—let it cook first.
✔ Variations: Add potatoes, hard-boiled eggs, or coconut milk for a twist.
✔ Storing: Tastes even better the next day!
