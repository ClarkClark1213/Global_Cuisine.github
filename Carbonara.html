CARBONARA

Authentic Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins | Total Time: 25 mins Servings: 4


Ingredients:

400g (14oz) spaghetti or bucatini
150g (5oz) guanciale (or pancetta), diced
4 large egg yolks + 1 whole egg
100g (1 cup) grated Pecorino Romano
50g (½ cup) grated Parmigiano Reggiano
Freshly ground black pepper
1 tsp salt (for pasta water)

Instructions:

1.Prepare Ingredients:
Mix egg yolks, whole egg, grated cheeses, and generous black pepper in a bowl.
2.Cook Pasta:
Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente (reserve 1 cup pasta water).
3.Crisp the Guanciale:
While pasta cooks, sauté guanciale in a large pan until crispy (no added oil needed).
Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
4.Combine Everything:
Drain pasta, add to guanciale pan (off heat).
Quickly mix in egg-cheese mixture, tossing constantly.
Add pasta water 1 tbsp at a time until creamy (about ¼ cup total).
5.Serve:
Plate immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.

Tips:
✔ Use room temperature eggs to prevent scrambling
✔ The pan must be off-heat when adding eggs to avoid curdling
✔ Authentic carbonara never uses cream or garlic
✔ For extra richness, use only yolks (no whole egg)
