Ratatouille

Classic French Ratatouille Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 60 mins | Total Time: 90 mins Servings: 6


Ingredients:

1 large eggplant (about 500g), diced
2 medium zucchinis (about 300g), sliced
2 red bell peppers, diced
4 ripe tomatoes (or 400g canned)
1 large onion, thinly sliced
4 cloves garlic, minced
3 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp herbes de Provence
1 bay leaf
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

1.Prep Vegetables:
Salt eggplant cubes and let sit 15 mins to remove bitterness. Rinse and pat dry.
2.Sauté Base:
In large pot, heat 2 tbsp oil. Cook onions until soft (5 mins), add garlic (1 min).
3.Layer Vegetables:
Add peppers, cook 5 mins. Then add zucchini and eggplant, cook 10 mins.
4.Simmer:
Add tomatoes, herbs, bay leaf. Cover and simmer on low heat 40 mins.
5.Finish:
Uncover, cook 10 more mins to thicken. Remove bay leaf, adjust seasoning.
6.Serve:
Drizzle with remaining olive oil, garnish with fresh basil.

Tips:
✔ For "Disney-style" presentation: Thinly slice veggies and arrange in spirals
✔ Better next day - flavors deepen when rested
✔ Serve with crusty bread or as side to grilled fish
✔ Substitute thyme/rosemary if herbes de Provence unavailable
