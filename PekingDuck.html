PEKINGDUCK

Authentic Peking Duck Recipe Prep Time: 24+ hrs (including drying) | Cook Time: 90 mins | Total Time: 26+ hrs Servings: 4-6


Ingredients:

1 whole duck (5-6 lbs), head and feet removed
3 tbsp honey
2 tbsp Chinese rice wine (Shaoxing)
1 tbsp white vinegar
2 tsp five-spice powder
1 tbsp grated ginger
6 cups boiling water
For Serving:
12 Mandarin pancakes (store-bought or homemade)
1 cup hoisin sauce
1 cucumber (julienned)
6 scallions (cut into matchsticks)

Instructions:

1.Prepare Duck:
Rinse duck, pat dry. Separate skin from meat by gently blowing air under skin (using a pump).
2.Glaze:
Mix honey, rice wine, and vinegar. Brush over duck, inside and out.
3.Dry:
Hang duck in cool, dry place for 12-24 hours (fan helps).
4.Roast:
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Pour boiling water over duck to tighten skin.
Roast breast-up on rack for 45 mins, flip, roast 45 mins more.
5.Crisp Skin:
Increase heat to 400°F (200°C) for final 10-15 mins until golden.
6.Serve:
Carve at table: slice skin first, then meat. Serve with pancakes, hoisin, and veggies.

Tips:
✔ Traditional method uses a duck hook and air pump for perfect crispiness
✔ No oven? Try a vertical beer can roaster for even cooking
✔ Save bones for amazing duck stock
✔ For shortcut: Some Chinese markets sell pre-prepared ducks
✔ Leftovers? Make duck fried rice or bao buns
