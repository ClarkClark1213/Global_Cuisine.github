BIRYANI

Fragrant Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani Recipe Prep Time: 60 mins (plus marination) | Cook Time: 50 mins | Total Time: 110 mins Servings: 6


Ingredients:

For Marination:
1.5 lbs (700g) chicken (bone-in pieces)
1 cup yogurt
2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp turmeric
2 tbsp red chili powder
1 tbsp garam masala
Juice of 1 lemon
For Rice:
3 cups basmati rice (soaked 30 mins)
1 bay leaf
4 cloves
2 cardamom pods
1 cinnamon stick
Salt to taste
For Layering:
3 large onions (thinly sliced, fried golden)
1/2 cup chopped mint
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
1/4 cup fried cashews
1/4 cup raisins
1/2 cup saffron milk (pinch of saffron in warm milk)
4 tbsp ghee

Instructions:

1.Marinate Chicken:
Combine all marinade ingredients with chicken. Refrigerate 2+ hours (overnight best).
2.Prep Rice:
Boil soaked rice with whole spices until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
3.Cook Chicken:
In heavy pot, layer half the fried onions, then marinated chicken. Cook covered 15 mins.
4.Layer Biryani:
Top chicken with half rice, then herbs, nuts, remaining onions. Repeat layers.
Drizzle saffron milk and ghee. Seal pot with foil before lid (dum cooking).
5.Slow Cook:
Cook on lowest heat 25 mins. Let rest 10 mins before serving.
6.Serve:
Mix gently, garnish with boiled eggs and extra herbs. Serve with raita.

Tips:
✔ Perfect rice texture: Each grain should stay separate
✔ Authentic dum cooking: Place tawa (griddle) under pot to prevent burning
✔ For veg biryani: Substitute with paneer/mixed vegetables
✔ Color variation: Use beetroot juice for red layers
✔ Leftovers taste even better next day as flavors meld
