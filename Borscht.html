BORSCHT

Classic Ukrainian Borscht Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 1.5 hrs | Total Time: 2 hrs Servings: 6-8


Ingredients:

For the Broth:
1 lb (450g) beef shank or short ribs
12 cups water
1 bay leaf
5 black peppercorns
For the Soup:
3 medium beets (peeled)
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 large onion, diced
2 carrots, grated
1 celery root, diced (or 2 celery stalks)
3 potatoes, cubed
½ small cabbage, shredded
2 tbsp tomato paste
2 tbsp white vinegar
1 tsp sugar
Salt to taste
For Serving:
Sour cream
Fresh dill
Garlic cloves
Rye bread

Instructions:

1.Prepare Broth:
Simmer meat with peppercorns and bay leaf 1.5 hrs. Skim foam. Strain, reserve meat.
2.Prep Beets:
Grate 2 beets, cube 1 beet. Keep separate.
3.Sauté Vegetables:
Cook onions in oil until golden. Add carrots and celery, cook 5 mins.
4.Build Soup:
Add cubed beets and potatoes to broth. After 10 mins, add cabbage.
5.Add Flavor:
Stir in grated beets, tomato paste, vinegar and sugar. Simmer 15 mins.
6.Finish:
Shred cooked meat, return to pot. Adjust seasoning.
7.Serve:
Garnish with sour cream, dill and crushed garlic. Serve with rye bread.

Tips:
✔ For vibrant color: Add grated beets at the end
✔ Traditional touch: Bake beets wrapped in foil before adding
✔ Vegetarian version: Use mushroom broth instead of beef
✔ Better next day - flavors deepen overnight
✔ Freezes well (without sour cream garnish)
✔ Secret ingredient: A splash of beet kvass for extra tang
✔ Drink pairing: Kompot or dark Ukrainian beer
