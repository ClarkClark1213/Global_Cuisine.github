KOTTBULLAR

Authentic Swedish Köttbullar Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins | Total Time: 50 mins Servings: 4-6


Ingredients:

For the Meatballs:
1 lb (450g) ground beef
½ lb (225g) ground pork
1 small onion, finely grated
½ cup breadcrumbs
¼ cup milk
1 egg
1 tsp salt
½ tsp black pepper
½ tsp allspice
¼ tsp nutmeg
2 tbsp butter (for frying)
For the Cream Sauce:
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
1½ cups beef broth
½ cup heavy cream
1 tsp soy sauce
½ tsp Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper to taste
For Serving:
Lingonberry jam
Mashed potatoes or boiled new potatoes
Pickled cucumbers
Fresh parsley

Instructions:

1.Prepare Meatball Mixture:
Mix breadcrumbs with milk and let soak. Combine all meatball ingredients.
2.Form Meatballs:
Roll into small balls (about 1" diameter). Chill 15 mins for firmness.
3.Cook Meatballs:
Brown in butter in batches (don't overcrowd). Set aside.
4.Make Sauce:
In same pan, make roux with butter and flour. Whisk in broth and cream.
5.Combine:
Return meatballs to sauce. Simmer 10 mins until cooked through.
6.Serve:
Garnish with parsley. Serve with potatoes, jam and pickles.

Tips:
✔ Authentic size: Make small meatballs (about 1 inch)
✔ For lighter version: Use half ground turkey
✔ Traditional touch: Grind your own meat mixture
✔ Freeze raw: Shape and freeze before cooking
✔ Ikea-style: Add 1 tbsp lingonberry jam to the sauce
✔ Leftover idea: Make meatball sandwiches next day
✔ Drink pairing: Swedish julmust or light lager
