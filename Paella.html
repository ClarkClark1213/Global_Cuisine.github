PAELLA

Authentic Spanish Paella Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins | Total Time: 60 mins Servings: 6


Ingredients:

For Sofrito Base:
3 tbsp olive oil
1 large onion, diced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 red bell pepper, diced
2 tomatoes, grated (or ½ cup canned crushed)
1 tsp smoked paprika (pimentón)
For Paella:
2 cups Spanish bomba or calasparra rice
4 cups chicken/fish stock (warm)
1 pinch saffron threads (about 20 threads)
1 lb (450g) chicken thighs, bone-in
½ lb (225g) rabbit (or extra chicken)
½ lb (225g) fresh green beans
½ lb (225g) large shrimp (with heads if possible)
½ lb (225g) mussels or clams
1 lemon, cut into wedges
Salt to taste

Instructions:

1.Prepare Sofrito:
Heat oil in paella pan. Sauté onion, garlic, bell pepper until soft (8 mins).
Add tomato and paprika, cook until thickened (5 mins).
2.Brown Meats:
Season chicken and rabbit with salt. Brown in pan (5 mins per side). Remove.
3.Toast Rice:
Add rice to pan, stir 2 mins to coat with oil. Dissolve saffron in warm stock.
4.Add Liquids:
Pour in stock, arrange meats and green beans. Do not stir after this point.
5.Cook:
Simmer 15 mins on medium-low. Arrange seafood on top last 5 mins.
6.Rest & Serve:
Remove from heat, cover with foil, rest 5 mins. Garnish with lemon.

Tips:
✔ Use a proper paella pan (30-38cm for this recipe)
✔ Never stir after adding stock - the crispy bottom (socarrat) is prized
✔ Substitute options: Chorizo for meat, squid rings for shellfish
✔ Traditional method: Cook over open flame for authentic char
✔ Leftovers reheat well but lose socarrat texture
✔ Wine pairing: Spanish Albariño or dry Rosé
