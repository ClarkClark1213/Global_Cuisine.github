KOSHARI

Authentic Egyptian Koshari Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 60 mins | Total Time: 90 mins Servings: 6


Ingredients:

For the Base:
1 cup brown lentils, rinsed
1 cup medium-grain rice
1 cup elbow macaroni
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tsp cumin
For the Tomato Sauce:
2 tbsp olive oil
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
1 tbsp vinegar
1 tsp sugar
½ tsp cayenne pepper
For the Crispy Onions:
2 large onions, thinly sliced
½ cup vegetable oil
For Serving:
1 can (15oz) chickpeas, drained
1 tbsp garlic vinegar (2 cloves minced in ¼ cup vinegar)
1 tsp ground cumin
Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

1.Cook Lentils:
Simmer lentils in 3 cups water for 20 mins until tender. Drain.
2.Prepare Rice:
Sauté rice in oil with cumin for 2 mins. Add 2 cups water, simmer 15 mins.
3.Cook Pasta:
Boil macaroni until al dente. Drain and toss with 1 tsp oil.
4.Make Tomato Sauce:
Sauté garlic in oil until golden. Add tomatoes, vinegar, sugar and spices. Simmer 15 mins.
5.Fry Onions:
Fry onion slices in hot oil until deep brown. Drain on paper towels.
6.Prepare Chickpeas:
Mix chickpeas with garlic vinegar and cumin.
7.Layer & Serve:
In bowls: rice → lentils → pasta → chickpeas → sauce → onions. Serve with hot sauce.

Tips:
✔ Authentic touch: Use small Egyptian macaroni (ditalini)
✔ For crispier onions: Soak slices in ice water 10 mins before frying
✔ Make ahead: Components keep 3 days separately
✔ Vegetarian protein: Contains complete protein from rice+lentils
✔ Egyptian style: Serve with extra vinegar sauce on side
✔ Leftover idea: Mix all components and pan-fry as koshari cakes
✔ Drink pairing: Hibiscus tea (karkadeh) or sugarcane juice
