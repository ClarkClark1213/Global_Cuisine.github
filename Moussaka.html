MOUSSAKA

Authentic Greek Moussaka Recipe Prep Time: 45 mins | Cook Time: 60 mins | Total Time: 105 mins Servings: 8


Ingredients:

For Eggplant Layers:
3 large eggplants (about 2 lbs), sliced ½" thick
Salt for sweating
Olive oil for brushing
For Meat Sauce:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 large onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1.5 lbs ground lamb (or beef)
1 can (14oz) crushed tomatoes
2 tbsp tomato paste
½ cup red wine
1 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp allspice
1 bay leaf
Salt and pepper to taste
For Béchamel:
4 tbsp butter
½ cup flour
3 cups warm milk
2 egg yolks
½ cup grated Parmesan
Pinch of nutmeg

Instructions:

1.Prep Eggplant:
Salt eggplant slices, let sweat 30 mins. Rinse and pat dry.
Brush with oil, broil 5 mins per side until golden. Set aside.
2.Make Meat Sauce:
Sauté onion and garlic in oil. Add lamb, brown thoroughly.
Stir in tomatoes, paste, wine, and spices. Simmer 20 mins.
3.Prepare Béchamel:
Melt butter, whisk in flour to make roux. Gradually add milk.
Cook until thickened. Remove from heat, stir in yolks and cheese.
4.Layer:
In 9x13" dish: eggplant → meat → repeat. Top with béchamel.
5.Bake:
Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 45 mins until golden and bubbly.
6.Serve:
Let rest 20 mins before cutting. Serve warm with salad.

Tips:
✔ Salting eggplant removes bitterness and reduces oil absorption
✔ For lighter version: Bake eggplant instead of frying
✔ Make ahead: Assemble 1 day before baking
✔ Traditional touch: Add sliced potatoes as base layer
✔ Leftovers taste even better next day
✔ Freezes well (without béchamel topping)
