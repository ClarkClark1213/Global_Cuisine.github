FEIJODA

Authentic Brazilian Feijoada Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins (plus overnight soak) | Cook Time: 3-4 hrs | Total Time: 4.5 hrs Servings: 8-10


Ingredients:

For the Stew:
2 lbs (900g) black beans, soaked overnight
1 lb (450g) pork shoulder, cubed
1 lb (450g) carne seca (dried beef), soaked and cubed
1/2 lb (225g) linguiça or chorizo sausage
1/2 lb (225g) smoked bacon, cubed
1 smoked pork hock or trotter
1 pig's ear (optional, traditional)
2 bay leaves
4 garlic cloves, minced
2 onions, chopped
3 tbsp olive oil
8 cups water or beef stock
For Serving:
White rice
Farofa (toasted cassava flour)
Sliced oranges
Collard greens, sautéed
Hot pepper sauce

Instructions:

1.Prepare Meats:
Soak salted meats (carne seca, pork hock) for 24 hours, changing water 3-4 times.
2.Cook Beans:
Drain soaked beans, cover with fresh water. Simmer with bay leaves 1 hour.
3.Brown Meats:
In separate pan, brown all meats in batches. Reserve rendered fat.
4.Make Sofrito:
In same pan, sauté onions and garlic in reserved fat until golden.
5.Combine:
Add meats, sofrito, and enough stock to cover. Simmer 2-3 hours until tender.
6.Adjust:
Season with salt if needed (meats may be salty enough). Skim excess fat.
7.Serve:
Traditionally served in clay pots with all accompaniments.

Tips:
✔ For shortcut: Use pressure cooker (1 hour total)
✔ No carne seca? Substitute with smoked beef brisket
✔ Skim foam during first hour for clearer broth
✔ Best made 1 day ahead - flavors improve overnight
✔ Traditional sides: Caipirinha cocktails and cachaça
✔ Vegetarian version: Use smoked tofu and mushrooms
