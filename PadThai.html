PHADTHAI

Authentic Pad Thai Recipe Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 10 mins | Total Time: 30 mins Servings: 2


Ingredients:

For Sauce:
3 tbsp tamarind paste
3 tbsp palm sugar (or brown sugar)
2 tbsp fish sauce
1 tbsp lime juice
For Noodles:
200g (7oz) rice stick noodles (medium width)
2 tbsp vegetable oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
100g (3.5oz) firm tofu, cubed
1 egg
100g (3.5oz) shrimp or chicken (optional)
1 cup bean sprouts
2 green onions, chopped
2 tbsp crushed peanuts
For Garnish:
Lime wedges
Extra bean sprouts
Fresh cilantro
Chili flakes (optional)

Instructions:

1.Prepare Noodles:
Soak noodles in warm water for 15-20 mins until flexible but firm. Drain.
2.Make Sauce:
Mix tamarind, sugar, fish sauce and lime juice until sugar dissolves.
3.Cook Protein:
Heat oil in wok. Add garlic, tofu and shrimp/chicken (if using). Cook 2 mins.
4.Add Egg:
Push ingredients aside, crack egg in center. Scramble lightly, then mix in.
5.Combine:
Add noodles and sauce. Toss 2-3 mins until noodles absorb sauce.
6.Finish:
Add bean sprouts and green onions. Toss 30 sec more.
7.Serve:
Top with peanuts, lime wedges, and extra sprouts. Serve immediately.

Tips:
✔ Soak noodles al dente - they'll finish cooking in the wok
✔ Authentic taste requires tamarind (not ketchup substitute)
✔ High heat is crucial for proper wok hei (breath of the wok)
✔ Vegetarian? Use soy sauce instead of fish sauce
✔ No wok? Use largest frying pan possible
✔ Leftovers don't keep well - make fresh each time
