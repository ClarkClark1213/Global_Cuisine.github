PHO

Authentic Vietnamese Pho Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 4-6 hrs | Total Time: 5-7 hrs Servings: 6-8


Ingredients:

For the Broth:
3 lbs beef bones (marrow and knuckle)
2 lbs beef brisket or flank
1 large onion, halved
4-inch ginger piece, halved lengthwise
5 star anise
6 cloves
1 cinnamon stick
1 tbsp coriander seeds
1 tbsp fennel seeds
1 black cardamom pod (optional)
1/4 cup fish sauce
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp salt
For Serving:
1 lb rice noodles (banh pho)
1/2 lb raw beef sirloin (thinly sliced)
1 onion, thinly sliced
3 scallions, chopped
1/2 cup cilantro
1/2 cup Thai basil
2 limes, cut into wedges
2-3 chili peppers, sliced
Bean sprouts
Hoisin sauce
Sriracha sauce

Instructions:

1.Prepare Bones:
Boil bones 5 mins, drain and rinse thoroughly (removes impurities).
2.Char Aromatics:
Broil onion and ginger until blackened (about 10 mins).
3.Toast Spices:
Dry toast spices in pan until fragrant (2 mins).
4.Simmer Broth:
Combine all broth ingredients in pot. Simmer 4-6 hours, skimming foam.
5.Prep Noodles:
Soak rice noodles in warm water 20 mins, then drain.
6.Slice Meat:
Slice cooked brisket and raw sirloin paper-thin.
7.Assemble:
Blanch noodles, top with meats. Pour boiling broth over to cook raw beef.
8.Serve:
Garnish with herbs, sprouts, lime and sauces to taste.

Tips:
✔ Clear broth secret: Parboil bones and skim constantly
✔ For shortcut: Use pressure cooker (90 mins cooking time)
✔ Traditional cut: Eye of round for raw beef topping
✔ Vegetarian option: Use mushroom broth and tofu
✔ Broth freezes well for future meals
✔ Leftover idea: Make pho fried rice next day
✔ Drink pairing: Vietnamese iced coffee or jasmine tea
