KEBAB

Authentic Middle Eastern Kebab Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins (plus 2 hrs marination) | Cook Time: 15 mins | Total Time: 45 mins Servings: 4


Ingredients:

For the Kebabs:
1.5 lbs (700g) ground lamb (or 50/50 lamb-beef mix)
1 small onion, grated
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tbsp ground sumac
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp smoked paprika
½ tsp ground cinnamon
½ tsp allspice
¼ tsp cayenne pepper
¼ cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp salt
For Serving:
4 pita breads
1 cup tahini sauce
1 cup chopped tomatoes
1 cup chopped parsley
½ cup pickled turnips
1 red onion, thinly sliced
Sumac for sprinkling
Lemon wedges

Instructions:

1.Prepare the Mixture:
Combine all kebab ingredients in bowl. Mix vigorously for 5 mins until sticky.
2.Form Kebabs:
Divide into 8 portions. Mold onto flat metal skewers in long sausage shapes.
3.Chill:
Refrigerate 2+ hours (overnight best) for flavors to develop.
4.Grill:
Preheat grill to high. Cook kebabs 3-4 mins per side until charred.
5.Serve:
Slide off skewers onto warmed pita. Top with veggies and tahini.
6.Traditional Presentation:
Roll up or serve open-faced. Sprinkle with sumac and lemon juice.

Tips:
✔ For juicier kebabs: Use 20% fat meat
✔ No skewers? Form into patties (kebab burgers)
✔ Authentic touch: Add 2 tbsp grated lamb fat to mixture
✔ Vegetarian option: Use spiced mushroom/eggplant mixture
✔ Grill alternative: Use broiler or cast-iron grill pan
✔ Leftover idea: Make kebab wraps next day
✔ Drink pairing: Ayran yogurt drink or mint lemonade
