MEATPIE

Classic Meat Pie Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 45 mins | Total Time: 75 mins Servings: 6


Ingredients:

For the Filling:
1 lb (450g) ground beef
1 onion, finely diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 carrot, grated
1 celery stalk, diced
1 cup beef stock
2 tbsp tomato paste
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp dried rosemary
1 tbsp flour
Salt and pepper to taste
For the Pastry:
2 sheets store-bought puff pastry (or homemade)
1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
Optional Extras:
1/2 cup frozen peas
1/2 cup diced potatoes
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

Instructions:

1.Prepare Filling:
Brown beef in pan, then add onion, garlic, carrot and celery. Cook 5 mins.
2.Thicken Filling:
Stir in flour, then add stock, tomato paste and seasonings. Simmer 15 mins until thickened.
3.Prepare Pastry:
Line pie dish with one pastry sheet. Trim edges.
4.Assemble Pie:
Add cooled filling to pastry. Cover with second sheet. Seal edges with fork.
5.Bake:
Brush with egg wash. Cut steam vents. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 25-30 mins until golden.
6.Serve:
Let rest 10 mins before slicing. Serve with mashed potatoes or salad.

Tips:
✔ For flaky pastry: Keep ingredients cold and handle minimally
✔ Prevent soggy bottom: Blind bake base for 10 mins first
✔ Freezer-friendly: Assemble unbaked pie and freeze for later
✔ Shortcut: Use pre-cooked roast beef leftovers
✔ Variations: Add cheese to pastry or mushrooms to filling
✔ Leftover idea: Reheat slices for breakfast with eggs
✔ Drink pairing: Full-bodied red wine or dark ale
