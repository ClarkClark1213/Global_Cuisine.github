BURGER

Classic American Burger Recipe Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 10 mins | Total Time: 25 mins Servings: 4


Ingredients:

1.5 lbs (700g) ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
4 hamburger buns (brioche or sesame seed)
4 slices American cheese
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
2 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)
Lettuce, tomato, onion slices for serving
Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise to taste
4 pickle slices (optional)

Instructions:

1.Prepare the Patties:
Gently mix ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder.
Form into 4 equal-sized patties (about ½ inch thick), making a slight indentation in the center.
2.Cook the Burgers:
Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side for medium (longer for well-done).
Add cheese slices during last minute of cooking to melt.
3.Toast the Buns:
Butter cut sides of buns and toast on grill/skillet until golden (about 1 minute).
4.Assemble:
Spread condiments on bottom bun.
Add lettuce, tomato, then burger patty with melted cheese.
Top with onions, pickles, and top bun.
5.Serve:
Serve immediately with fries or potato chips!

Tips:
✔ Don't overwork the meat - handle gently for juicier burgers
✔ Let patties rest at room temperature for 15 minutes before cooking
✔ For smash burgers: Press thin patties firmly onto hot griddle
✔ Alternative toppings: Bacon, mushrooms, barbecue sauce, or fried egg
