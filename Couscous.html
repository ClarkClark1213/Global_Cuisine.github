COUSCOUS

Traditional Moroccan Couscous Recipe Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 60 mins | Total Time: 90 mins Servings: 6


Ingredients:

For the Couscous:
2 cups medium-grain couscous
3 cups vegetable or chicken broth
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp turmeric
For the Vegetable Stew:
1 large onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 carrots, peeled and chopped
2 zucchini, chopped
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 cup pumpkin or butternut squash, cubed
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 can chickpeas, drained
3 tbsp tomato paste
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
½ tsp ground cinnamon
½ tsp ginger powder
Pinch of saffron (optional)
4 cups vegetable or chicken stock
½ cup raisins (optional)
Fresh cilantro and parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1.Prepare the Stew:
Heat oil in large pot. Sauté onion and garlic until soft (5 mins).
Add spices and tomato paste, cook 1 minute until fragrant.
2.Cook Vegetables:
Add carrots, bell pepper, and pumpkin. Cook 5 mins.
Pour in stock, bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer 15 mins.
3.Add Remaining Vegetables:
Add zucchini, tomatoes, and chickpeas. Simmer 15 mins more.
4.Prepare Couscous:
Place couscous in large bowl. Mix with 1 tbsp oil and salt.
Gradually add 2 cups warm broth, fluff with fork. Cover 10 mins.
5.Steam Couscous (Traditional Method):
Transfer couscous to steamer basket over simmering stew.
Steam 15 mins, fluffing occasionally with fork.
6.Serve:
Mound couscous on platter, make well in center for stew.
Garnish with herbs and raisins if using.

Tips:
✔ For perfect texture: Fluff couscous with fork, never stir
✔ Traditional touch: Use a couscoussier for authentic steaming
✔ Protein options: Add lamb, chicken or merguez sausage
✔ Quick version: Use instant couscous (skip steaming step)
✔ Leftover idea: Make couscous salad with lemon dressing
✔ Drink pairing: Serve with sweet mint tea
